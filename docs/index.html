About Me

I am a (mostly) web developer who is passionate about building a better web for everyone, and consequently the businesses that power it.

Whether through technical innovation, solid user experience or process management.

I've had the fortune of many years of experience building technical solutions using a wide range of technologies, from Actionscript (way-back machine alert) through to Ruby on Rails.

My core focus of late, has been front-end development, as that is where the rubber hits the road in terms of good or bad user experience.
